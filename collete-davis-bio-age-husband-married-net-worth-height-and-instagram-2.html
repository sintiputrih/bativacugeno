    Collete Davis is an American professional racecar driver, stunt driver, entrepreneur, and national TV Host. Davis went to school at Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University for Mechanical Engineering and entrepreneurship at Sixteen and moved to Silicon Valley at eighteen to be mentored by VC Tim Draper at Draper University.

    With numerous wins under Davis’s belt across multiple forms of racing, Davis’s now competing in sports cars, drift competitions and is the new driver of the 12,000lb Wonder Woman Monster Jam truck. In the year 2017, Davis was the host of ‘Girl Starter’, a competition TV show for female entrepreneurs on TLC. Davis is also one of Microsoft’s (People of Action) where she promotes STEM education and women empowerment internationally with the organization.

    Davis is driven to popularize STEM for the masses and share her story with the world to encourage female empowerment in male-dominated industries where Davis constantly tries to challenge the very notion of what a woman can be in today’s world
