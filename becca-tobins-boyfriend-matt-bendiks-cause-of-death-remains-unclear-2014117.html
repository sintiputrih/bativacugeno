    The cause of death of Glee star Becca Tobin's boyfriend, Matt Bendik, has yet to be determined, even after an autopsy was conducted, a spokesman for the Philadelphia Medical Examiner's Office tells Philly.com.

    As previously reported, Bendik was found dead in his Philadelphia hotel by a hotel housekeeper on July 10. 

    The spokesman said in an email to Philly.com the autopsy did not immediately establish a clear cause of the 35-year-old nightclub entrepreneur's death, adding, however, no evidence of trauma was found. 

    The Medical Examiner's Office will be conducting additional studies, "as is the usual procedure in evaluating a sudden, unexpected death," the spokesperson concluded.
