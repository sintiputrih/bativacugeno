    Max Martin is a Swedish music producer and songwriter who rose to fame in the 1990s for writing hit songs such as "As Long As You Love Me," "Baby One More Time," and "I Want it that Way." Born on February 26, 1971 in Stockholm, Sweden, Martin was recognized for his work at the 2015 Grammy's, where he was awarded Non-Classical Producer of the Year.

    About
