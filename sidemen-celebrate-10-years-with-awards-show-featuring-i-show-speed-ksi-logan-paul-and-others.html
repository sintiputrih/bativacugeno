    LONDON, UK: The Sidemen, a UK-based YouTube collective, celebrated their remarkable journey in the world of online content creation by marking a decade of success with a special one-hour mukbang video on their official YouTube channel.

    As part of their anniversary festivities, the group organized a unique awards ceremony to recognize outstanding achievements and memorable moments within their community.

    Honoring a decade of YouTube stardom

    For those unacquainted with The Sidemen, the group was established in 2013 and consists of well-known YouTubers and streamers, including KSI, Harry Wroetoshaw, Miniminter, Vikkstar123, Behzinga, TBJZL, and Zerkaa.

    Over the years, they have grown to become one of the largest and most influential YouTube collectives with a dedicated following of over 20 million subscribers.

    The Sidemen's anniversary celebration featured a special one-hour mukbang video, allowing fans to join in on the festivities and watch their favorite creators indulge in delicious food while reflecting on their journey in the online world.
