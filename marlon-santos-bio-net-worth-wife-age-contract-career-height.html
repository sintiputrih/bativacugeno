    Marlon Santos is a Brazilian professional footballer who is currently playing for the Ukrainian club, Shakhtar Donetsk. His playing position is center-back for his current team. He joined the Fluminense academy at the age of 14 and since then, he has played for the Barcelona B team, French club Nice, and Italian Serie A club Sassuolo. On 22 June 2021, Shakhtar Donetsk announced the signing of Marlon to a five-year contract. He won the 2021 Ukrainian Super Cup while playing for FC Shakhtar Donetsk.

    Marlon Santos Career Timeline

    • Marlon joined the Fluminense academy at the age of 14 through the youth ranks. 
    • He then made his debut for Fluminense at the age of 18, in the Campeonato Brasileiro Série A, versus São Paulo in a 5–2 victory. 
    • After three months and six days, he played in the Copa Sudamericana. 
    • He made 51 appearances for Fluminense until he was loaned out on a season-long loan to the Barcelona B team. 
    • Next, he signed for Barcelona on a season-long loan on 8th June 2016. 
    • He made his debut for the main squad of Barcelona on 23 November 2016 in a Champions League Group C game against Celtic. 
    • He made his debut in La Liga against Las Palmas.
    • He was signed by Barcelona from Fluminense for €5 million signing a four-year contract.
    • In August 2017, the club, Nice signed Marlon Santos on a two-year loan with an option to buy at the end of the loan for €20 million and made his debut for Nice against Monaco in a 4–0 win. 
    • After, his loan ended early at the end of the 2017–18 season; he signed to Italian Serie A club Sassuolo for an initial fee of €6 million with a buy-back clause for Barcelona on 16th August 2018. 
    • On 22nd June 2021, Shakhtar Donetsk announced the signing of Marlon to a five-year contract, and currently, he is playing for the team.
