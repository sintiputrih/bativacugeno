    Big Brother 25 Spoilers: 4 Houseguests Names Dropped, Janelle Pierzina Blasts BB25 Cast As Boring

    Big Brother 25 (BB25) spoilers tease that the new season will be one to remember. Social media is buzzing about the cast and rumors that one of the houseguests was kicked off the show to avoid fan backlash.

    22 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Big Brother 25 (BB25) spoilers tease that the new season will be one to remember. Social media is buzzing about the cast and rumors that one of the houseguests was kicked off the show to avoid fan backlash.

    The official CBS cast announcement should come out in the next few days. Big Brother host Julie Chen revealed that CBS is airing a celebration episode on Wednesday, July 26, to kick off the season, with the premiere episode airing on Wednesday, August 2.

    The cast names leaked so far are Jared Martin, Constance Ruiz, Joanna Watson, and Brian Chiman. There are no details about the cast members other than their names and photos.

    Another juicy tidbit revealed on social media was that one of the houseguests, who was a former beauty pageant contestant, was removed from the show after Big Brother discovered a series of transphobic social media posts.

    The network didn’t want to risk having someone in the house with a history of bullying the LGBTQ community.

    Big Brother legend Janelle Pierzina didn’t hold back when she saw the rumored cast members, calling them boring and corny.

    “Just saw three rumored houseguests for #bb25, and they look pretty corny and boring,” Janelle wrote on Twitter. She added that she’s heard that a few of the cast members have “huge followings” on social media.

    Pierzina suggested this could be another season with a 12-person alliance on the first night, with the whole house voting together week after week. She’s ready to watch a season of surprises, triple evictions, and other surprises. Janelle is growing tired of the predictable cast and season twists.

    Big Brother viewers also expressed their opinions and hope the season brings back everyday people battling it out for $750K.

    The BB25 fans do not want to see people with huge social media followings appearing on the show to future their careers as social media influencers.

    Big Brother 25 spoilers say we should know all 16 names of the houseguests later this week.
