    Meet Gregory Kelleher, Emma Hernan Ex

    Gregory Kelleher is Emma Hernan’s ex. The two were spotted in a now-deleted photo posted to Emma’s mother’s Instagram page, where they were seen sharing a kiss by the seashore.

    Since the photo was shared back in 2014, it makes sense that Emma might have gotten engaged to her ex more than five years ago.

    Now, as for the link between Gregory and Christine Quinn, there were no credential records of them being together. However, Christine claiming the two were a couple is enough to establish a link between them. Moreover, even Emma admitted that she know that Gregory and Christine were together.

    The last we saw Christine and Emma, they were shouting at each other over their “mutual” ex.

    But it’s also possible that Christine’s ex “Gregory” may have been a different guy altogether.

    If you ask us, there should be no reason for the Selling Sunset stars to have that awkward connection, since they’ve long moved on. 

    Christine went on and married the multi-millionaire teach mogul, Christian Richard, while Emma was still in search of that one person who’d give her kids.

    Does Gregory Kelleher Have A Wife Now?

    Yes, Gregory Kelleher has a wife named Cara Beneduce. He married her on August 20, 2021, in Newport, while surrounded by their family and friends.

    Cara is a Warwick, Rhode Island native who attended East Carolina University. She joined the Tri Sigma sorority back in September 2013 and remained an active member ever since.

    The duo enjoyed the outdoors and mostly loved camping. It wasn’t just them who enjoyed the adventures. They also had a god who often tagged along in their trips.

    Reportedly, the pair also owned a cat named Tilly who went missing in October 2020 in Warwick.

    Here’s Cara’s IG @cara_jeanne, and Facebook @cara.beneduce.

    Gregory Kelleher Age

    Gregory Kelleher was reportedly born before 1988. So, he couldn’t have been any younger than 33 years of age in 2021.

    As for his wife, Cara turned 26 on September 13, 2021. On her birthday that year, Gregory took it to his IG with a series of pictures from their wedding captioned, “Happy birthday to my Beautiful Wife! @cara_jeanne and I tied the knot a few weeks ago, we had an amazing weekend in Newport with family and friends!”

    Gregory Kelleher Job

    Gregory Kelleher is a general contractor by profession. He owned a construction company called GK Construction LLC based in Maryland. The company mentioned that it was built on the principles of making quality products and providing reliable service.

    “Our diversified services range continues to grow by following trends, improving our standard products, and listening to the customer,” their official page wrote.

    Before starting his own company, Gregory worked at Liberty Construction Services (joined in November 2013). Also, he worked as an assistant superintendent at NEI General Contracting before that.

    In case you want to know, an average general contractor in the US made around $85 thousand per annum.
