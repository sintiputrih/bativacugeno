    Early Years and Upbringing

    Both Seth and Stephen Curry grew up in the city of Charlotte, North Carolina as two sons of an illustrious basketball family. They were born to their father, Dell Curry, a former NBA player with the Charlotte Hornets, and their mother Sonya instilled in them right from the start his love for basketball. It was in the basketball-crazed city of Charlotte that these two young brothers first honed the raw potential they each possessed for developing into stars at some point down the line.
    Growing up, Seth and Stephen both idolized their father's successful NBA career. Dell Curry will always be the Charlotte Hornets' all-time leading scorer and most prolific three-point shooter, which is a lasting legacy of how he impacted basketball. After all, their mother Sonya Curry - herself a former Division I volleyball star - played an integral part in instilling the discipline and mentality that would eventually define how they approached basketball.
