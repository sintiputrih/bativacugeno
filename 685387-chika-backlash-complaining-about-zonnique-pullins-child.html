    Chika recently took to social media to air her complaints about children seated next to her on a flight. She vented about the twins' behavior, calling out their mother for bringing them on the plane in the first place. Zonnique Pullins responded to her posts, revealing that the children in question were hers. She responded to the rapper's comments, telling her, "You should [have] just sat there and been mad babe..she sky priority af," adding a cheeky emoji.

    Chika went off on the mother in her recent Twitter rant. She wrote, "The lady next to me who thought it would be a good idea to buy yourself and your twin infants first class seats on a red eye flight, who just woke me up by bringing your screaming bastard to OUR seats to soothe her, i just bought $34 wifi at 4 am to call you a stupid B**ch." She went on to tell Zonnique she hates her, calling her a "senseless wench" and asking her if she's "mentally delayed."

    Chika Apologizes For Her Rant

    Chika received near-instant backlash for her comments. She later went back to Twitter to apologize to those offended by the rant. "I'm sorry cuz it was triggering/infuriating to many," she wrote, "I just don't like being seen as someone I'm not." The rapper, however, later shared an Instagram post where she further discussed the incident and the backlash she received.

    "I know it was real harsh but sorry, I cant give a f**k," Chika explained. She went on to claim that at the time of her posts she hadn't slept in two days, and was having "really bad thoughts." She then cited "double-standards," claiming that despite throwing anything her way, haters are quick to be on her case. "We all go through it we all say things," Chika claimed. "I don't feel bad cuz the response is disproportionate to the mistake."
