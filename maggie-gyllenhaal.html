    Maggie Gyllenhaal is an American actor, film producer, director, theatre actor, and voice artist who is the daughter of filmmakers Stephen Gyllenhaal and Naomi Achs and the older sister of fellow actor Jake Gyllenhaal. She is known for playing Lee Holloway in the erotic black comedy romantic drama film Secretary, Rachel Dawes in the superhero film The Dark Knight, Ana Pascal in the fantasy comedy-drama film Stranger than Fiction, Eileen “Candy” Merrell in the television drama series The Deuce, Isabel Green in the period fantasy comedy film Nanny McPhee and the Big Bang, Jude in the dramedy film Happy Endings, Elizabeth “Zee” (voice) in the computer-animated supernatural comedy horror film Monster House, Clara in the independent black comedy film Frank, Jean Craddock in the drama film Crazy Heart, Debbie in the biographical spy film Confessions of a Dangerous Mind, Sherry Swanson in the drama film Sherrybaby, and Nessa Stein, Baroness Stein of Tilbury in the political spy thriller series The Honourable Woman.
