    Overall Meaning

    "Jose Cuervo" is a classic country song by Shelly West that is beloved by fans for its catchy chorus and relatable lyrics about the aftermath of a night of excessive drinking. The song tells the story of a woman who wakes up with a hangover after a wild night of partying and drinking tequila with salt and lime, courtesy of her friend Jose Cuervo. She is struggling to piece together the events of the previous night and wonders if she kissed all the cowboys, started a fight or danced on the bar.


    Throughout the song, the singer's memory is hazy, and the events of the previous night are foggy. She is surprised to find a cute cowboy sleeping beside her and struggles to remember how she got his shirt on, all of which suggests that she had a wild night that was fueled by her love of tequila.


    The song cleverly captures the feeling of being hungover and trying to piece together what happened the previous night. It also highlights the powerful and sometimes humorous effects of tequila on people. Overall, "Jose Cuervo" is a fun, lighthearted song that is perfect for a night out with friends or a karaoke session.
