    Priyanka Chopra is one of the many excited fans celebrating the royal baby's arrival today. The actress and close friend to Meghan Markle gave the new bundle of joy a sweet shoutout on her Instagram Story this afternoon. She posted a screenshot of the Duke and Duchess's announcement and wrote, "Congratulations M & H," referencing Meghan and Prince Harry. She also threw in a pink heart sticker on the graphic.

    Instagram

    Harry and Meghan first broke the news of their firstborn's arrival this morning with an Instagram post reading, "It's a boy! Their Royal Highnesses the Duke and Duchess of Sussex are overjoyed to announce the birth of their child."

    In the caption, the proud new parents announced that their son weighs seven pounds and three ounces. "The Duchess and baby are both healthy and well, and the couple thank members of the public for their shared excitement and support during this very special time in their lives," the message continued. "More details will be shared in the forthcoming days."

    Chopra previously celebrated the Sussex's baby news when Meghan's pregnancy was first announced in October. At the time, she joked to E! News, "I just have a few of my friends who are having babies right now and I'm like, 'God I need to catch up!'"

    The Quantico star is expected to attend the Met Gala in NYC tonight, along with her husband Nick Jonas, whom she brought as a date to the high-fashion event two years ago. Perhaps she'll spill some intel about the new royal baby on the red carpet.
