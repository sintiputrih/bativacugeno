    White Lotus Actress Eleonora Romandini Wiki | Bio

    Eleonora Romandini (born on February 23, 1993) is a 29-year-old Italian actress commonly recognized for the role of Commessa she played in the 2019 TV series “La Compagnia del cigno.” In 2022, this pretty artist made it into the headlines after joining the cast of Mike White’s comedy-drama “The White Lotus” as Isabella.

    For those who do not know, this HBO Max show revolves around the employees and guests at a Hawaiian resort named White Lotus. Here you will get to watch Jennifer Coolidge, Jon Gries, Alexandra Daddario, and Connie Britton in the lead roles.

    Do you know that besides being an accomplished actress, Romandini is also a lawyer? Yes, it is true the actress has a Doctor of Law Degree from Università Statale Di Milano. Keep reading to find more interesting facts about this Italian beauty.

    Where was Eleonora Romandini Born?

    On February 23, 1993, Eleonora Romandini (age 29), in Milan, Lombardy, Italy. Although there aren’t much data available about Ele’s family, we found a photograph where she can be seen with her parents and her sister, Alessandra Romandini.

    Today we recognize Eleonora as a successful actress, but there is a fascinating story behind her acting journey. According to Nuvola, the actress used to learn musical instruments as a child and did that as a hobby; she had never thought of becoming an artist. Her mom and dad believed that she must get a college degree and not risk her career in acting or music.

    Following her parents’ advice, she started her study for Law Degree at Università Statale Di Milano. One day one of her friends, who was attending IED, asked her if she wanted to be in a short movie; she said yes. While working with her friend on a video, Romandini met a guy from an advertisement agency who offered her a job in a TV commercial, which she accepted.

    Eleonora Romandini’s Acting Career

    While working on the TV commercial Eleonora realized that she wanted to be an actress, so she started her acting classes but kept studying for her law degree. In 2016, Roma stepped into the small-screen business with “Trailers.” Soon after, she appeared as Tiffany in Maccio Capatonda’s mini-series “Mariottide.”

    Her next project came three years later in 2019; it was a family drama where Ivan Cotroneo filmed Romandini alongside Anna Valle, Alessio Boni, Alessandro Fella, and Barbara Chichiarelli. Meanwhile, she also worked on a short film named “Rondini” with director Riccardo Petrillo. A little later, she received the offer to work on a movie titled “Corro da te” where she worked with “World War Z” actor Pierfrancesco Favino.

    The year 2022 proved to be the best for this Italian actress’s career because she got to display her talent through five engaging productions, including “The General’s Men,” “Màkari,” “The White Lotus,” and “Il grande Giorno.”

    Who is Eleonora Romandini’s Boyfriend?

    Romandini regularly shares posts on her Instagram account (@eleonoraromandini), which has around 4k followers.

    While scrolling through her IG handle, we found a photograph in which we could see her with a guy who seemed like her partner. Upon digging more, we learned that the guy we saw in the photo is Romandini’s boyfriend, Davide Enea Casarin, who is a model/actor by profession.
