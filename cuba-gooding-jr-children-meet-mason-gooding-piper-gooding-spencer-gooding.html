    Cuba Gooding Jr. Children: Meet Mason Gooding, Piper Gooding, Spencer Gooding

    22 Sep 2024, 00:00

    American actor Cuba Gooding Jr. has three children Mason Gooding, Piper Gooding and Spencer Gooding. Cuba Gooding Jr. is a proud father and has supported his children in their pursuits, whether it be acting or maintaining a private life away from the public eye.

    Meet Spencer Gooding

    Spencer Gooding is Cuba Gooding Jr.’s eldest son. He was born on November 19, 1994.  Spencer, who graduated from Wesleyan University in Connecticut like his father, has worked as an actor and made his television debut in a 2002 episode of SlamBall.

    According to his Instagram page, he has also worked as an associate editor at WEBTOON and streams video games on Twitch.

    Meet Mason Gooding

    Mason Gooding, born on November 14, 1996, is Cuba Gooding Jr.’s second son. He has followed in his father’s footsteps and pursued a career in acting. Mason has appeared in films such as “Booksmart” (2019) and the 2022 film Scream. Mason previously studied dramatic writing and psychology at New York University Tisch School of the Arts before dropping out during his junior year to pursue acting full-time.

    Meet Piper Gooding

    Piper Gooding is Cuba Gooding Jr.’s daughter. She was born on October 22, 2005. As with Spencer, not much information is publicly available about Piper, as the family maintains a level of privacy regarding their personal lives.
