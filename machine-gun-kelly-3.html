    Career

    Machine Gun Kelly started his career as a rapper at a very early age.

    In the year 2006, he released the debut mixtape titled Stamp of Approval.

    He has gained huge recognition and stage name Machine Gun Kelly after his first release due to his rapid-fire delivery.

    After that, he gained huge popularity and had consecutive victories at the Apollo Theatre in 2009. Later, he released his second mixtape titled 100 Words and Running, in the same year he also released his debut single titled Alice in Wonderland.

    After that, he released many popular singles like Wild Boy, Invincible, Stereo, Hold On, Till I Die, and many others. He also released several popular albums like Lace-Up, General Admission, Bloom, Hotel Diablo, and Tickets to My Downfall. He also released many mixtapes and settled himself as one of the popular rockstars in the United States and across the globe.

    In 2014, he made his acting debut with his appearance in the popular movie titled Beyond the Lights. He also worked in some other movies like The Land, Roadies, Bird Box, The Dirt, and Big Time Adolescence. He also worked in some popular television series like WWE Raw, Guy Court, Unsung, Catfish, Roadies, and others.

    He has also been a part of popular bands like AJ Tyus, Steve Baze Basil, JP Rook Cappelletty, and Travis Barker. In the year 2011, he was also featured in the magazine named XXL. He also worked with Bad Boy Records and Interscope Records.
