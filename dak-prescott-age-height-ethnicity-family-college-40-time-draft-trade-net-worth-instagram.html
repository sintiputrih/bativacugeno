    Rayne Dakota Prescott is an American football quarterback for the Dallas Cowboys of the National Football League (NFL).

    Table of Content

    Age and birthday

    He was born on July 29, 1993, and is 29 years old.

    Nationality and ethnicity

    He was born in Sulphur, Louisiana, and identifies as an American.

    Height and weight

    He stands at 6 ft 2 in or 1.88 m, and weighs 238 lb or 108 kg.

    College

    He committed to play college football at Mississippi State University over offers from Louisiana Tech, LSU, Memphis, Nicholls State, North Texas, and TCU.

    There he stood out and ended his career with numerous accolades to his name. These include: the Conerly Trophy, the Davey O’Brien Award, the Johnny Unitas Golden Arm award, and the Manning Award

    Draft and Trade (When was he drafted?)

    n the fourth round (135th overall)in the 2016 NFL Draft, he was selected by the Dallas Cowboys.

    40‑yard dash

    His 40 yard dash time is 4.79 s.

    Family

    He was born to Nathaniel and Peggy Prescott and has four siblings; two older brothers, Tad and Jace, an older sister, Natalie Prescott-Smith, and an older half-brother, Elliott Prescott.

    His mother Peggy passed away from colon cancer in November 201 and his older brother Jace committed suicide in April 2020.

    Salary and net worth

    He is estimated to be worth between $40 million and $60 million.
