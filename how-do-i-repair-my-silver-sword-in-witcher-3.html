    To repair your silver sword in Witcher 3, you have a few options:

    Use a Repair Kit

    Repair Kits are a usable item that can be purchased at every Blacksmith or Armorer. These kits are useful for when you are out in the field and need to repair your silver sword during a battle. Simply use the item in your inventory and apply it to the silver sword that needs repairing.

    Visit a Blacksmith or Armorer

    You can also visit a Blacksmith or Armorer to have your silver sword repaired. They can repair it for you from within the crafting menu. Simply talk to the Blacksmith or Armorer and request them to repair your silver sword.

    Use a Weapon Repair Kit

    If you have found some “weapon repair kits,” you can apply them to your silver sword to restore some of its functionality. These kits can be obtained as loot or purchased from various merchants throughout the game.
