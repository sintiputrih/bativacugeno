    Overall Meaning

    In Riperton Minnie's song Reasons, she shows her appreciation for the various reasons that have given meaning to her life. The first verse indicates that the million faces represent the promises that have been made to her, which she can sense in her bones but are slipping away from her grasp. However, the joy of singing with all her might creates a sense of wonder and comfort. She also reassures her listeners that they are not alone in their struggles.


    In the second verse, the reasons for her life are buried in deep recesses and can only be found through words that will evoke them. In the face of madness and oppression, she perseveres, and the reasons for her life cannot be bought or sold. The reasons have filled her spaces and are like rushing waters that flow and guide her, untangling her from the burdens she carries. Despite not knowing all the reasons for her life, she is grateful for the ones she possesses.


    The song speaks to the idea that life is full of promises, experiences, and emotions that define us. The reasons for our lives, whether they are apparent or buried deep within us, make our existence meaningful. The song encourages listeners to appreciate the beauty of the small things that make life worth living and to find comfort in the fact that they are never alone.
