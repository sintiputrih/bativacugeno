    Frank C. Turner is an actor and iconographer born in Wainwright, Alberta and now living in Port Coquitlam, British Columbia. He has acted in theatres across western Canada and Ontario, and since 1983 has mainly worked in film. His credits include Air Bud, The New Addams Family, Lonesome Dove, Cats & Dogs, Snow Dogs, and The Duke. He has studied iconography under Vladislav Andreyev, Charles Rohrbacher, Mary Katsilometes, Cathy Sievers, and Father Gianluca Busi. He is a founding member of Epiphany Sacred Arts Guild and has served as its president for four years, and has also served on the curriculum advisory board of Living Waters College.
