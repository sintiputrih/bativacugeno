    Biography

    John Newman was born in Settle, England on June 16, 1990. Soul and pop singer best known for his chart topping single “Love Me Again,” which was released as part of his debut album Tribute. He was nominated for Best British Male at the 2014 BRIT Awards. In 2013, he appeared on Jools Holland‘s Hottenanny and Holland’s Rhythm and Blues Orchestra. When he was 20, he moved to London, where he started his first band and was subsequently signed by Island Records.
