    T. J. Holmes Facts

  • His nickname at home was T. Jr., but he changed it to T. J. after starting his career on television.
  • Holmes was added to The Root 100 List of ‘Most Influential Black Americans’ in 2011.
  • The breaking news covered by him include the Deepwater Horizon oil spill in 2010, 10th anniversary of the September 11th attacks in 2011, Pope Benedict’s visit to the United States in 2008, Saddam Hussein‘s execution in 2006, terrorist attacks in Mumbai in 2008, and Queen Elizabeth II‘s platinum jubilee in 2022.
