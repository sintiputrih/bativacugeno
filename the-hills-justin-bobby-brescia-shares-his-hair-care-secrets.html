    'The Hills' Justin Bobby Brescia Shares His Hair Care Secrets

    Justin Bobby Brescias hair routine is just as chill and laid-back as he is. But showing off his gorgeous, tousled locks, it clearly works and works well for The Hills star. Then again, he is a trained hairstylist.

    17 Feb 2024, 00:00

    Justin Bobby Brescia’s hair routine is just as chill and laid-back as he is. But showing off his gorgeous, tousled locks, it clearly works and works well for The Hills star. Then again, he is a trained hairstylist.

    Before the 37-year-old was showing us all that “the rest was still unwritten” on TV, he trained at Paul Mitchell the School and Vidal Sassoon to become a hairstylist. In fact, it was at the salon he was working at when he met his on-again off-again girlfriend on the show, Audrina Patridge, who was working as a receptionist there.

    During his career as a hair guru he’s worked with some big names like Adam Levine and Madonna. He even launched his own hair care line, Brush Your Hair, which is an organic and vegan professional line that houses products like shampoo, conditioner and hair wax.

    When The Hills cast stopped by the Us Weekly offices, he talked a little about his go-to routine, his hair care secret and products he swears by.

    So how exactly does he keep his hair so luscious, shiny and flawlessly wavy? You’ll have to watch the video to find out! But we will say his routine in general is very — we repeat very — minimal. He shared how little he washes his hair, which helps him “shape and be natural with it.” He even told Us a major step he skips that most of Us do without even thinking about it.

    However, it’s the one no-no piece of advice that he abides by that we’re taking with Us. “No heat,” he said. “Don’t add heat to your hair.”
