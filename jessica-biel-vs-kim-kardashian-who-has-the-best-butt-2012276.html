    In the battle of the A-list backsides, Kim Kardashian can’t be beat!

    According to new search data released by Bing.com, 21 percent of site users searched for information related to the 31-year-old Keeping Up With the Kardashians star’s derriere and her workout routine as they prepped for summer bikini season.

    Coming in second was none other than Jennifer Lopez, 42, with the American Idol judge and mom of 4-year-old twins’ bootylicious behind accounting for 13 percent of all search traffic over the last three months.

    Jessica Biel — who showed off her toned bikini bod June 22 on vacation in Puerto Rico — was the third most searched celebrity, with users clamoring to know more about how the future Mrs. Justin Timberlake scored her slammin’ derriere.
