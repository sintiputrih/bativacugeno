    Dominik Mysterio’s character growth is nothing short of remarkable, as he wasn’t taken seriously as a babyface. His fortune changed after he turned to the dark side and is now one of the biggest heels in Vince McMahon’s company. Now it appears he has a profane response to fans who come up to him to tell them about their birthdays.

    The Judgment Day is comprised of Finn Balor, Damian Priest, Rhea Ripley and Dominik Mysterio. Together, they have been wreaking havoc in WWE. Rhea Ripley and Mysterio have seen the most growth during their time in the faction.

    The NXT North American Champion took to social media to send a message to fans who come up to him, saying it’s their birthday. He simply used a gif which stated: “F*** your birthday.”

    When people come up to me and say “hey it’s my birthday today” expect this.

    Dominik Mysterio will not be wrestling at WWE Payback this Saturday, but the rest of his Judgment Day stablemates will. We’ll have to wait and see what’s next in store for Mysterio.
